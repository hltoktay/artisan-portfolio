United Artisans' Medium is an arts & crafts collective aiming to revive the ideal of craftsmanship in an age of increasing mechanisation, mass production and critically endangered crafts. We collaborate with highly skilled crafts people and master artisans from different regions and backgrounds creating mostly unique or limited-edition handmade works.

At United Artisans' Medium, we firmly believe in ethical and sustainable production, respecting our workers and leaving behind a smaller footprint on our planet.
